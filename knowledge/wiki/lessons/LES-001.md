## Content

- Event: the guardian's secret-pattern check BLOCKed twice on credential-shaped strings used as markers in test fixtures. Both were fake, both were correctly flagged — the diff could not prove they were fake.
- Source: PRs #33 and #36 preparation (local guardian runs).
- Category: guardian-catch
- Lesson: never put credential-shaped strings in fixtures or docs; use markers that are lexically un-secret-like (`fake-...-do-not-leak`). Fix the string, never the rule.
- Loop feed: validated the existing secret-pattern rule (true positives by design); fixture naming practice adopted.
- Status: closed — consumed by fixture renames in both packages; rule unchanged and re-validated.
