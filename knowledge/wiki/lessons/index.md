## ID allocation (tandem sessions — LES-L02)

Lesson ids are partitioned by session to avoid the sequential-counter add/add collision two concurrent sessions otherwise race for: the **laptop session** allocates `LES-L01, LES-L02, …`; the **cloud session** keeps the default `LES-NNN` sequence. Already-merged ids keep their numbers. Any new shared monotonic id (work items, migrations) under concurrent authorship follows the same rule: a per-session namespace or a single allocator, never optimistic first-come.
