# Research Engine

## Purpose

The Research Engine gathers, ranks, summarizes, and preserves engineering evidence before decisions are made.

## Source Priority

1. Official documentation
2. Standards and RFCs
3. Maintained reference implementations
4. Benchmarks and papers
5. Security advisories
6. Maintainer discussions
7. Community reports and opinions

Community opinion must be clearly labeled as opinion.

## Research Techniques

- official docs first
- compare 3 to 5 alternatives
- inspect reference repos
- check license
- check maintenance velocity
- check issue health
- check security posture
- check integration complexity
- validate claims with examples
- separate facts from recommendations

## Outputs

- research dossier
- research note
- source quality score
- conflicting evidence
- open questions
- decision impact
- confidence

## Integration

Research feeds:

- Technology Fitness Engine
- Decision Intelligence
- Design Intelligence
- PR Guardian
- Continuous Research Engine
- Evolution Intelligence

## Principle

Research is reusable knowledge, not disposable search output.
